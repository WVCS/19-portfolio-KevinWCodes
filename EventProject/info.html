Eat The Soup!

THINGS TO BRING:

Remember to bring enough food and water for a week even though Eat The Soup! is only a 5-day event
Bring LOTS of sunscreen
You will need a tent and sleeping bags (it gets cold at night)
Bring your own toiletries (there will be public showers and latrines)
If you bring valuables make sure you have a safe space to hide or store them
BRING CHAIRS

THINGS TO REMEMBER:

This is a anarchic wonderland of free expression, NOT a place for children
There is always a risk of theft
If you need help, we have security stations and security personnel
There are shows every hour on the hour from 6AM to 11PM
Don't get in trouble, this event and its participants WILL adhere to all appropriate laws
We reserve the right to kick people from the event for little to no reason
On the last day we serve the communal soup

RULES:

NO consumption of drugs (unless prescribed with letter of consent from a licensed doctor of medicine)
NO alcohol
Comply with all laws, rules, and security personnel
NO fights unless the participants do so with pool noodles in a cage at a prescheduled time
NO messing with the performers
NO FULL NUDITY
NO contaminating the soup
You may bring homemade costumes
You may apply body paint
You may make artwork
Feel free to share our message over social media

BREAKING THESE RULES WILL RESULT IN A MINIMUM FINE OF $100 DOLLARS OR PROSECUTION TO THE FULL EXTENT OF THE LAW

SOUP BEARERS:

You will be required to wear special robes while serving the soup
You will be expected to show respect and kindness while serving the soup
You must fill out the form provided to become a soup bearer; some laws may determine who can serve
If the soup is tainted or expected to be tainted the soup bearers must stay at the festival for questioning
You are expected to stay at the festival grounds and uphold the Eat The Soup! motto
Be friendly and extend our greetings to others

INGREDIENT BEARERS:

This is the most prestigious job at the event
Contact an administrator for a sign-up form (must be submitted before February 1st)
You may either
*Pay us a fee and we will allow you to bring your own ingredients (which we then process)
*Or tell us an ingredient and we will buy it
You will not be paid
You will be expected to uphold the Eat The Soup! motto
