Welcome to the Guinea Pig Project Home Page



This Website is designed to bring joy to those who view it by explaining what guinea pigs are and why people own them as pets, showing pictures of guinea pigs, informing the readers on guinea pig habits, diet, and other interesting facts, introducing them to the life of a rodent caretaker, and finally by explaining the different kinds of guinea pig breeds and the care that they require to be kept happy and healthy.


This Website was created by Kevin Wetherell for entertainment and informational purposes only. Use this resource to learn the basics in guinea pig care!
